Art & Architecture

Art and architecture are interconnected disciplines that both express human creativity and shape our environment. Architecture, as a form of art, can be viewed as a blend of artistic expression, technical skill, and functional design. Art, in turn, can be integrated into and inspired by architecture, enriching the visual and experiential landscape of our built environment.

History

The history of architecture encompasses the evolution of buildings and structures across various civilizations and periods. From early human shelters to complex modern designs, architecture reflects cultural, social, and technological advancements. Key periods include ancient civilizations (like Egypt and Greece), the Roman Empire, the Middle Ages, the Renaissance, and Modernism.
